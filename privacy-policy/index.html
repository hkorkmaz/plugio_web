Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Plugio ("we", "our", or "us") develops visualization gadgets for Atlassian Jira, including gauge, counter, and progress bar widgets. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our Jira plugin (the "Plugin").

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our Plugin is a client-side visualization tool that renders data already present in your Jira instance. We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any Jira project data, issue data, or any other content from your Jira instance.
  • We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.
  • We do not send any data to external servers.
  • We do not process data outside of your Jira environment.

3. How the Plugin Works

The Plugin operates entirely within your Atlassian Jira instance. It reads data from Jira using the standard Atlassian gadget APIs and renders visual representations (gauges, counters, and progress bars) directly in your browser. All processing happens locally within the Jira environment — no data ever leaves your Jira instance through our Plugin.

4. Data Storage

Plugio does not maintain any databases, servers, or external storage systems for user data. The Plugin's configuration (such as which metrics to display) is stored within Jira's own dashboard configuration system, managed entirely by Atlassian.

5. Third-Party Services

The Plugin does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services. It operates solely within the Atlassian Jira platform using Atlassian's provided APIs.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Plugin does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Since we do not collect any user data, we have no means of directly notifying users of changes — we encourage you to review this page periodically.
