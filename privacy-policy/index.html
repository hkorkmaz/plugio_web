Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Plugio ("we", "our", or "us") develops visualization plugins and widgets for productivity platforms, including gauge, counter, and progress bar widgets. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our plugins (the "Plugin").

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our Plugin is a client-side visualization tool that renders data already present in your platform instance. We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any project data, task data, or any other content from your platform instance.
  • We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.
  • We do not send any data to external servers.
  • We do not process data outside of your platform environment.

3. How the Plugin Works

The Plugin operates entirely within your platform instance. It reads data using the platform's standard APIs and renders visual representations (gauges, counters, and progress bars) directly in your browser. All processing happens locally within your platform environment — no data ever leaves your instance through our Plugin.

4. Data Storage

Plugio does not maintain any databases, servers, or external storage systems for user data. The Plugin's configuration (such as which metrics to display) is stored within the platform's own dashboard or configuration system, managed entirely by your platform provider.

5. Third-Party Services

The Plugin does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services. It operates solely within your platform using the platform's provided APIs.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Plugin does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Since we do not collect any user data, we have no means of directly notifying users of changes — we encourage you to review this page periodically.
